About Me

About Me

An Indian singer and songwriter. She was one of the contestants in the television reality show Indian Idol Season 10. She also showed her talent on the stage of SA RE GA MA PA 2021 and emerged as the winner of the show Indian Idol 10.



"Neelanjana Ray is an Indian singer. She became popular after becoming the winner of singing reality show Sa Re Ga Ma Pa 2022 (Season 30). She has participated in number a number of singing reality shows including Voice India Kids Season 2."

"Neelanjana was inclined towards music since childhood. She started practising singing at a very young age."

"In 2017, she appeared as a contestant on Voice of India Kids Season 2, a singing reality competition television series for kids."

"Next in 2018, Neelanjana participated in Sony Entertainment Televisions singing reality show Indian Idol Junior Season 10. She was the 2nd runner up for the show and was rewarded with a sum of Rs 1 Lakh."
